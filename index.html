About

Nix Mix is the newest addition to the Jamaica Diner/Bar scene. Run by Nick Varsos, owner of Nix Mix Cafe, it is no wonder why this diner is one of the new hottest spots to do anything, from having a family brunch to getting drinks after last call.

The diner is equipped with a full bar serving their famous Nix Mix beer blend, and is open all day & night. The diner holds a nostalgic atmosphere with furnishings inspired by classic 60s diner vibes, with a contemporary touch of color-changing lights, mirror-lined walls, and all laid out in a unique space.
